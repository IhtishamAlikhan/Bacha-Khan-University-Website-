University Introduction

BACHA KHAN UNIVERSITY, CHARSADDA was established on July 03, 2012 with the mission to advance knowledge and learning through quality research and education for the whole nation. The message of peace and universal brotherhood as practiced and preached by Abdul Ghaffar Khan (Bacha Khan) will be the motto of the University to steer the organization in the years ahead which would induct Pakistan into the comity of respectable nations of the world. Prof. Dr. Fazal-ur-Rahim Marwat was appointed as the first Vice Chancellor of the University.

Vision:

To be a prominent public sector University in the region having international reputation in research and creative thinking and to provide quality education and encourage research activities of socioeconomic development of the area and the country at large, with the motto of "March towards knowledge based economy."

Mission:

To foster creative and critical thinking among the students with a view to promote their intellectual growth and to equip them with knowledge, skills and par excellent attitude to deliver the highest standards of higher education. To promote scientific research to acquire excellence in various fields of study. To establish effective network and coordination with leading national, regional and international institutions to share experiences of academic and research excellence
